## 🚀 New Models, Datasets & Open Weights

**ByteDance Lance 3B — Unified Any-to-Any Multimodal (Apache-2.0)**

ByteDance's Intelligent Creation Lab released **Lance** (May 19/21), a 3B-active-parameter native unified multimodal model supporting text-to-image, text-to-video, image editing, video editing, image understanding, and video understanding — all in a single framework. Architecture: dual-stream mixture-of-experts on shared interleaved multimodal sequences, decoupling understanding and generation pathways. Trained from scratch on 128 A100 GPUs; runs within 40 GB VRAM. Published under Apache-2.0 with weights on HuggingFace and GitHub. Currently the **#2 trending model on HuggingFace** (trending score 721, 812 likes). Paper: arXiv:2605.18678. — [bytedance-research/Lance](https://huggingface.co/bytedance-research/Lance) (direct) / [MarkTechPost, 2026-05-21](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/05/21/one-model-three-modalities-bytedance-releases-lance-for-image-and-video-understanding-generation-and-editing/) [via snippet]

**Tencent Hy-MT2 — 33-Language Translation Suite (Open Weights, Three Sizes)**

Tencent open-sourced three **Hy-MT2** multilingual translation models on HuggingFace and ModelScope (May 11–21): **1.8B**, **7B**, and **30B-A3B** (MoE, 3B activated), covering 33 languages plus five Chinese dialects. Key engineering details: Tencent's AngelSlim quantization compresses the 1.8B variant to 440 MB while accelerating inference; GGUF and 4-bit quants are available; models are optimized for ARM and Intel silicon for on-device deployment. Hy-MT2-1.8B is currently **#1 trending on HuggingFace** (score 723, 763 likes). — [tencent/Hy-MT2-1.8B](https://huggingface.co/tencent/Hy-MT2-1.8B) (direct) / [Phemex News, 2026-05-21](https://phemex.com/news/article/tencent-hunyuan-opensources-hymt2-models-launches-translation-mini-program-83974) [via snippet]

**EU AI Act: High-Risk Compliance Deadline Extended 16 Months to December 2027**

On May 7, EU co-legislators reached a provisional political agreement under the **"Digital Omnibus VII"** package that significantly revises the AI Act implementation schedule. Key changes: **Annex III high-risk AI systems** (hiring tools, credit scoring, biometric identification, critical infrastructure management) now face obligations by **December 2, 2027** rather than August 2, 2026 — a 16-month extension. **AI-generated content transparency obligations** (marking synthetic outputs) pushed to **December 2, 2026** (+4 months). National regulatory sandbox requirements pushed to August 2027. Annex I product-regulated systems (medical devices, lifts) see a 1-year extension to August 2028. Formal adoption expected June 2026; publication July. Companies that already built compliance programs for August 2026 are navigating an awkward reprieve. — [EU Council press release, 2026-05-07](https://www.consilium.europa.eu/en/press/press-releases/2026/05/07/artificial-intelligence-council-and-parliament-agree-to-simplify-and-streamline-rules/) [via snippet] / [Latham & Watkins Inside Privacy](https://www.insideprivacy.com/artificial-intelligence/eu-ai-act-update-timeline-relief-targeted-simplification-and-new-prohibitions/) [via snippet]

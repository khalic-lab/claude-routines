## 🔬 Science (bio, neuro, physics, astronomy, math)

- **Anesthetized human hippocampus decodes language and predicts upcoming words.** Baylor College of Medicine researchers recorded hippocampal neurons in unconscious patients under general anaesthesia and found them detecting auditory "oddball" tones (with improving accuracy over time, suggesting neural plasticity), distinguishing parts of speech in running stories, and anticipating upcoming words via predictive coding. Challenges the assumption that sophisticated language processing requires consciousness; may enable new speech-prosthetic approaches. Published in _Nature_. [Nature, 2026 — "Plasticity and language in the anaesthetized human hippocampus"](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-026-10448-0) [via snippet] + [BCM press release, May 2026](https://www.bcm.edu/news/researchers-discover-advanced-language-processing-in-the-unconscious-human-brain) [via snippet]

- **Single HPV vaccine dose protects Kenyan women for 4.5 years with 99.3% efficacy.** A randomised cross-over trial in Kenya found a single bivalent HPV dose conferred 99.3% (95% CI: 96.2–99.9%) protection against persistent HPV 16/18 infection at 4.5 years — the strains responsible for most cervical cancers. Supports WHO's single-dose schedule (adopted by Kenya in Nov 2025) and strengthens the case for simplified low-income-country rollout. Published in _Nature Communications_. [Nature Communications, 2026](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-026-72654-8) [via snippet]

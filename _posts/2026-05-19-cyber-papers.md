## 🛡️ Cybersecurity

- **CVE-2026-42945 "NGINX Rift" actively exploited — CVSS 9.2, heap buffer overflow enabling possible unauthenticated RCE.** A critical heap buffer overflow in `ngx_http_rewrite_module` affects NGINX Open Source versions 0.6.27 through 1.30.0 and NGINX Plus R32–R36 (pre-patch). The flaw was introduced in 2008 and allows a specially crafted HTTP request to crash worker processes; RCE is possible where Address Space Layout Randomization (ASLR) is disabled. VulnCheck canary systems began flagging exploitation attempts on 16 May — three days after AlmaLinux published patches and technical details. Patch to NGINX Open Source 1.30.1 or 1.31.0; NGINX Plus users need R32 P6 or R36 P4. Temporary mitigation: replace unnamed captures with named captures in affected `rewrite` directives. — [Help Net Security, 18 May 2026](https://www.helpnetsecurity.com/2026/05/18/ngnix-vulnerability-exploited-cve-2026-42945/) [via snippet] / [The Hacker News, 18 May 2026](https://thehackernews.com/2026/05/nginx-cve-2026-42945-exploited-in-wild.html) [via snippet] / [NVD](https://nvd.nist.gov/vuln/detail/CVE-2026-42945) [via snippet]

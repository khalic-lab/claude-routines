[via snippet] [McGill University Newsroom, May 2026](https://www.mcgill.ca/newsroom/channels/news/discovery-fat-burning-switch-could-lead-advances-bone-disease-treatments-372896) / [ScienceDaily, May 11 2026](https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/05/260511213141.htm)

Announced May 13. [single-source] [via snippet] [ScienceDaily, May 13 2026](https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/05/260513034640.htm)

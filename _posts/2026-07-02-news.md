## 🇨🇭 Switzerland & Vaud

- **Federal social-insurance projections turn grim: disability fund (IV/AI) reserves could run dry by 2031.** The Federal Social Insurance Office (OFAS/BSV) published updated financial projections Thursday warning that the **IV** — Switzerland's disability insurance — could see its reserves disappear as early as **2031**, with SRF reporting the scheme heading toward an **800-million-franc annual deficit**. The old-age pension (**AVS**, the state retirement pension, AHV in German) is under parallel pressure and needs durable financing for the newly approved **13th monthly payment**. This is the numbers behind a debate that directly shapes future payroll deductions and pension levels for anyone working in Switzerland. ([Le Temps, 2 Jul 2026](https://www.letemps.ch/articles/avs-sous-forte-pression-et-ai-au-bord-du-gouffre-les-nouvelles-projections-financieres-inquietent); [SRF, 2 Jul 2026](https://www.srf.ch/news/schweiz/sozialversicherungen-iv-steuert-auf-defizit-von-800-millionen-franken-zu))
- **EFTA and Vietnam sign a comprehensive free-trade agreement.** Switzerland, Iceland, Liechtenstein and Norway — the **EFTA** bloc (European Free Trade Association, the four non-EU Western European states) — concluded a deal that abolishes all Swiss tariffs on industrial goods and fish from Vietnam; Vietnam will phase out duties on watches, machinery, chemicals, cheese and chocolate over up to 11 years. Economics minister Guy Parmelin welcomed the deal, which lands as a rare win in a difficult trade week — parliament's lower house rejected the Mercosur pact last week and a US tariff framework expires end-July. Bilateral trade is ~CHF 2.5 bn (Vietnam is Switzerland's third-largest Southeast Asian partner), small next to the ~CHF 68 bn Swiss–US goods trade. ([SRF, 2 Jul 2026](https://www.srf.ch/news/schweiz/keine-zoelle-mehr-auf-fisch-efta-und-vietnam-unterzeichnen-freihandelsabkommen)) `[official PR]`

([Le Temps, 2 Jul 2026](https://www.letemps.ch/articles/christelle-luisier-isabelle-moret-et-frederic-borloz-les-trois-conseillers-d-etat-plr-veulent-rempiler-en-2027))

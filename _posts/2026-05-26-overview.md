[preprint] [arXiv:2605.24281, 2026-05-26](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.24281)

Light-curve modelling favours a ~12 M☉ progenitor with ~0.5 M☉ of circumstellar material; the host is a low-metallicity star-forming galaxy. [preprint] [arXiv:2605.24088, 2026-05-26](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.24088)

- **Ultrafast magneto-optics fingerprints altermagnetism's dual-space nature in MnTe** — Time-resolved pump-probe measurements on the altermagnet MnTe show coexisting Kerr (momentum-space Berry curvature) and Voigt (real-space Néel order) effects with distinct relaxation timescales — directly resolving the predicted simultaneous ferromagnet-like and antiferromagnet-like character in the same crystal. Positions altermagnets as platforms for ultrafast spin-optoelectronics. [preprint] [arXiv:2605.24320, 2026-05-26](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.24320)

- **Cavity vacuum fluctuations alone can flip superconductor from singlet to triplet pairing** — Theory shows that electromagnetic cavity vacuum fields (zero photons) reshape the Fermi surface in a polarisation-dependent way, altering competition among pairing channels. Above a critical light-matter coupling, the leading instability switches from singlet to triplet superconductivity — a phase absent in the bare material — raising prospects for engineering topological superconductivity without doping or pressure. [preprint] [arXiv:2605.24439, 2026-05-26](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.24439)

## 📄 ML research — first arXiv batch

- **arXiv:2605.23926** (2026-05-26) — *How Much Thinking is Enough? Quantifying and Understanding Redundancy in LLM Reasoning* — Finds that 61–93% of reasoning steps in frontier models are redundant: the correct answer is recoverable from a tiny prefix of the chain. Critically, **proves** this is a structural consequence of length-agnostic outcome rewards in RL — not a per-model bug — so over-thinking cannot be patched without changing training objectives. Direct implications for inference cost and reasoning-model design. [preprint] [Abstract](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.23926)

- **arXiv:2605.24033** (2026-05-26) — *Towards Verifiable Transformers: Solver-Checkable Circuit Explanations* — Closes a gap in mechanistic interpretability: circuit explanations are typically validated by ablation; this work encodes extracted circuits into SMT solvers so a plausible explanation can be formally verified or refuted. Demonstrates on GPT-style models and scales to surrogate-mediated verification at GPT-2 size. [preprint] [Abstract](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.24033)

- **arXiv:2605.24057** (2026-05-26) — *Feature Lottery? A Bifurcation Theory of Concept Emergence* — Uses dynamical-systems bifurcation theory to detect the precise training moments when neural networks acquire structured concept representations, label-free, via a universal phase coordinate computable from hidden states alone. Shows that SAE feature identity is predictable from only 5% of training, with top-decile atoms achieving 12x baseline purity at convergence. [preprint] [Abstract](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.24057)

- **arXiv:2605.24216** (2026-05-26) — *Agent-ToM: Learning to Monitor Autonomous LLM Agents via Theory-of-Mind Reasoning* — Applies Theory-of-Mind inference to detect covert malicious behaviour in autonomous agents that pursue hidden objectives while appearing benign across many turns — a hard safety problem previous monitoring approaches ignored by treating each trajectory independently. [preprint] [Abstract](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.24216)

- **arXiv:2605.24218** (2026-05-26) — *QUEST: Training Frontier Deep Research Agents with Fully Synthetic Tasks* — Releases open 2B–35B deep-research agents trained via a unified rubric-tree data-synthesis pipeline using only 8K synthetic tasks; matches or surpasses proprietary closed-source agents across eight benchmarks spanning fact-seeking, citation grounding, and report synthesis. [preprint] [Abstract](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.24218)

- **arXiv:2605.24173** (2026-05-26) — *Extracting Training Data from Diffusion Language Models via Infilling* — Shows that edge-conditioned masking on diffusion LMs extracts up to 3× more verbatim training sequences than prefix probing, and recovers redacted PII at higher rates than scale-matched autoregressive models.

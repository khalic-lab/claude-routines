**Anthropic Code with Claude London — self-hosted sandboxes and MCP tunnels** `[vendor PR]`

Anthropic's two-day London developer event (May 19–20) shipped two new features for Claude Managed Agents, with coverage landing today:

- **Self-hosted sandboxes** (public beta): companies can run agent tool calls on their own infrastructure or through managed providers (Cloudflare, Daytona, Modal, Vercel). The agent orchestration loop stays on Anthropic's infrastructure; only tool execution moves to the customer environment, keeping sensitive files and internal services off Anthropic's network.
- **MCP tunnels** (research preview): a lightweight end-to-end encrypted gateway connects agents to private-network MCP servers without inbound firewall rules or public endpoints — lets agents reach internal databases, private APIs, and ticketing systems as tools.

MIT Technology Review published analysis today framing both features as Anthropic hardening enterprise posture ahead of direct competition with AWS and Vercel for agentic compute. — [claude.com/blog, 19 May 2026](https://claude.com/blog/claude-managed-agents-updates) `[via snippet]` · [MIT Technology Review, 21 May 2026](https://www.technologyreview.com/2026/05/21/1137735/anthropics-code-with-claude-showed-off-codings-future-whether-you-like-it-or-not/) `[via snippet]` · [The New Stack, May 2026](https://thenewstack.io/anthropic-mcp-tunnels-sandboxes/) `[via snippet]`

## 💼 Industry, Funding, Regulation

**EU AI Act omnibus deal — high-risk AI delayed to December 2027**

On May 7, the Council of the EU and the European Parliament reached a provisional political agreement on targeted amendments to the EU AI Act (the "Digital Omnibus"). Key changes:

1. **Annex III high-risk AI** (employment, education, law enforcement, critical infrastructure use cases): application deadline moved from **August 2, 2026 → December 2, 2027**.
2. **Annex I high-risk AI** (AI embedded in regulated products, e.g. medical devices, machinery): deferred to **August 2, 2028**.
3. **GPAI watermarking obligations**: deferred to **November 2, 2026**.
4. **New prohibition**: AI systems that generate non-consensual intimate imagery ("nudifier" tools) and CSAM added to the prohibited-practices list.

The provisional agreement requires formal endorsement by both institutions and publication in the Official Journal before it becomes law. Given the original August 2026 deadline, an accelerated adoption timeline is expected. `[single-source for some provisions; corroborated for key delay by multiple law firm analyses]` — [EU Council press release, 7 May 2026](https://www.consilium.europa.eu/en/press/press-releases/2026/05/07/artificial-intelligence-council-and-parliament-agree-to-simplify-and-streamline-rules/) `[via snippet]` · [Lewis Silkin, 7 May 2026](https://www.lewissilkin.com/insights/2026/05/07/the-council-and-parliament-agree-to-slim-down-and-delay-parts-of-the-eu-ai-act-102ms0v) `[via snippet]`

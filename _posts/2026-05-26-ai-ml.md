## 🏢 Lab blogs & official releases

**Google SynthID goes cross-industry: OpenAI, Nvidia, ElevenLabs, and Kakao adopt AI content watermarking** `[vendor PR]`

Announced at Google I/O on May 19, SynthID — Google DeepMind's steganographic watermarking standard — has been adopted by four additional AI companies. OpenAI now embeds both a C2PA manifest and a SynthID watermark in every image generated by ChatGPT, Codex, and the OpenAI API. ElevenLabs applies SynthID to AI-generated audio; Kakao to image generation; Nvidia integrated it into its generative media pipeline. Google reports that SynthID has now been applied to over 100 billion pieces of generated media (images, video, audio), and that SynthID and C2PA Content Credentials verification are being integrated into Google Search, Chrome, and the Gemini app itself. The standard, which began as a DeepMind research project, is becoming a de facto content-provenance layer for the industry. — [Google Blog, May 2026 (via snippet)](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/products/identifying-ai-generated-media-online/); [ETV Bharat, May 2026 (via snippet)](https://www.etvbharat.com/en/technology/openai-nvidia-eleven-labs-and-more-to-adopt-googles-synthid-watermarking-technology-enn26052004851)

## 🚀 New models, datasets, & open weights

**MiniCPM5-1B — OpenBMB's 1B on-device LLM reaches new SOTA for its size class** `[Apache 2.0]`

OpenBMB (Tsinghua NLP Lab) shipped MiniCPM5-1B on May 19, 2026, paired with a companion desktop app (MiniCPM Desk Pet) generating fresh press today. Specs: 1B dense Transformer using standard LlamaForCausalLM — no custom kernels, no model-code fork, loads in any mainstream inference engine. Context window: 131K tokens. INT4 weights: ~0.5 GB, runnable on phones, browsers, and laptops. Benchmark composite: 42.57 averaged across reasoning, knowledge, code, instruction-following, math, logic, and agentic tasks — 20% above the prior 1B-class SOTA of 35.61, with the largest edges in agentic tool use, competition math, and code. A built-in `<think>` chat template supports hybrid (instant / deliberate) reasoning from the same checkpoint. Four HF variants are live: base, SFT, GGUF (llama.cpp / Ollama / LM Studio), and Apple Silicon-optimized. License: Apache 2.0. — [HF model page (direct, via HF API)](https://huggingface.co/openbmb/MiniCPM5-1B); [Gigazine, May 26, 2026 (via snippet)](https://gigazine.net/gsc_news/en/20260526-minicpm5-1b-openbmb-ai/)

**Supertonic-3 — 31-language on-device TTS at ~99M parameters** `[OpenRAIL-M]`

Supertone released Supertonic-3 on April 29, 2026 (HF upload May 6; MarkTechPost coverage May 15). Key advancement over v2: language coverage expands from 5 to 31 languages while reducing repeat and skip reading failures, improving cross-language speaker similarity. Model size: ~99M parameters across public ONNX assets (~404 MB download) — substantially smaller than the 0.7B–2B range of open TTS alternatives. Practical for edge deployment; includes a local OpenAI-compatible speech API. New feature: expression tags (`<laugh>`, `<breath>`, `<sigh>`) for more naturalistic prosody. License: OpenRAIL-M. 48K downloads and 690 likes on HF, currently #6 trending. — [Supertone/supertonic-3 HF page (via snippet)](https://huggingface.co/Supertone/supertonic-3); [MarkTechPost, May 15, 2026 (via snippet)](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/05/15/supertone-releases-supertonic-v3-on-device-text-to-speech-model-with-31-language-support-fewer-reading-failures-and-expression-tags/)

**CohereLabs Command-A-Plus-05-2026-w4a4 — W4A4 quantized multimodal flagship** `[Apache 2.0]`

Cohere Labs released `CohereLabs/command-a-plus-05-2026-w4a4` on May 18, 2026 — a W4A4 (4-bit weights, 4-bit activations) quantized version of its Command-A+ multimodal model, using the `cohere2_vision` architecture. W4A4 is among the most aggressive quantization regimes applicable to frontier-class models; it substantially reduces compute and memory cost at inference without custom training. Task: image-text-to-text (conversational). Currently #8 trending on HF (206 likes, 7.8K downloads in first week). `[single-source]` — [HF model page (direct, via HF API)](https://huggingface.co/CohereLabs/command-a-plus-05-2026-w4a4)

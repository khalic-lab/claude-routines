## 🌍 World politics — US morning

- **Trump cancels planned Iran strike, cites "serious negotiations."** President Trump announced Monday evening that he called off a US military attack on Iran that had been planned for Tuesday, at the explicit request of Gulf allies — Qatar, Saudi Arabia, and the UAE — who said they were "close to a deal." Trump said he had prepared "a very major attack" but paused it "for a little while, hopefully, maybe forever." Iran submitted a revised 14-point proposal to Pakistani mediators on Monday; key open issues include the Iranian nuclear programme, Strait of Hormuz transit rights, and sanctions relief. Iran's state television dismissed the announcement as a "retreat" based on "fear." Context: the US has blockaded Iranian ports since April 13; Hormuz closure has driven surging energy prices globally.

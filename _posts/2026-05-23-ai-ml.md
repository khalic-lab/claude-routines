## 🍎 Apple Silicon / On-Device

- **Ollama 0.19 MLX backend (March 2026) now standard for Apple Silicon.** Since March, Ollama has shipped its Apple Silicon inference backend on Apple's MLX framework rather than Metal + llama.cpp. Reported gains: 30–50% faster generation than llama.cpp on Metal, 1,851 token/s prefill and 134 token/s decode on int4 at the 8B-class scale. On M5-series hardware (M5, M5 Pro, M5 Max), Ollama additionally leverages the new on-GPU Neural Accelerators for improved time-to-first-token.

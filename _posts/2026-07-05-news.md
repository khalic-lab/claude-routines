## 🇨🇭 Switzerland & Vaud

- **The Federal Council wrapped its annual summer "course d'école" through canton Vaud on Friday (2026-07-03), hosted by Confederation President Guy Parmelin.** The traditional two-day outing took the seven-member cabinet across La Broye, La Côte and the Chablais — deliberately beyond the Lake Geneva shoreline — with public receptions at the castles of Aubonne and Aigle and a visit to Switzerland's second-largest flour mill at Granges-près-Marnand. - **The Swiss army says unidentified drones flew in formation over sensitive military sites — and it has no idea who is behind it.** Army chief Benedikt Roos disclosed that drones were seen over the Jassbach barracks in the Bernese Emmental — where the army trains cyber-defence and radio-reconnaissance (signals-intelligence) specialists and runs a listening station — and over the Meiringen air base, one of Switzerland's F-35 fighter locations.

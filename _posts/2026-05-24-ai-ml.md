## 🚀 New models, datasets & open weights

- **DeepSeek V4-Pro & V4-Flash** (released 2026-04-24, MIT licence). V4-Pro: 1.6T total parameters, 49B active per token (MoE), 1M-token context, 865 GB download. V4-Pro posts 80.6% on SWE-bench Verified, placing it alongside GPT-5.5 and Claude Opus 4.7 at the top of the open-weight agentic-coding tier. SWE-bench Verified: 85.4%, tying GPT-5.5 (85.1% per OpenAI claim); SWE-bench Pro: 58.6%. The "Agent Swarm" feature scales to 300 parallel sub-agents executing 4,000 coordinated steps simultaneously — 3× the capacity of its predecessor K2.5. Artificial Analysis ranks Kimi K2.6 fourth globally (Intelligence Index 54), behind Anthropic, Google, and OpenAI (all 57). - **ZAYA1-8B** by Zyphra (released 2026-05-06, Apache 2.0). 8.4B total parameters, ~760M active per token (MoE routing), trained end-to-end on AMD Instinct MI300X clusters on IBM Cloud — the only notable 2026 open-weight model not trained on NVIDIA or Huawei Ascend hardware. On mathematics benchmarks using the Markovian RSA methodology, ZAYA1-8B matches or exceeds Claude 4.5 Sonnet, Gemini-2.5-Pro, and DeepSeek-V3.2 despite being ~100× smaller by parameter count. Technical report: [arXiv 2605.05365](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05365) `[preprint]`. Model weights on HuggingFace; serverless endpoint on Zyphra Cloud. `[via snippet]` — [Zyphra blog, 2026-05-06](https://www.zyphra.com/post/zaya1-8b) | [PRNewswire, 2026-05-06](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/zyphra-releases-zaya1-8b-a-reasoning-model-trained-on-amd-and-optimized-for-maximum-intelligence-density-per-parameter-302764700.html) `[via snippet]`

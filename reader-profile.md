## Who the reader is
- Based in **Switzerland** (Europe/Zurich); cares about Swiss federal/cantonal politics and how
  EU–CH relations (Bilaterals III, free movement) actually affect life and work here.
- Works in **software / AI**; reads the AI-ML and Science briefs for signal, not hype.

## Favor (lead with these)
- **Primary sources and new developments** — labs, papers, official releases, court/gov filings —
  over aggregator restatements. A story is worth space when it *moves* (a number, a ruling, a
  release), not because it's trending.
- **Personal/local impact** — things that change a decision, a cost, or a rule for someone living
  and working in Switzerland get more space than equal-weight world news.

## Demote / cut
- Vendor PR and launch-marketing as a *lead* (fine as context).
- Re-summaries of already-covered stories with no new dated fact (see `tools/dedup/DEDUP.md`).

## Tone
- Explanatory and honest about uncertainty; name what's preview/rumour vs confirmed; disclose
  sourcing gaps. Better to omit than to dilute.
